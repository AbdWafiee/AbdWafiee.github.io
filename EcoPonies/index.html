A Friendly Welcome

We would like to wish Selamat Mikot/Selamat Sabi which means welcome to your first glimpse of Eco Ponies Garden.

We are a family-run farm-stay which is supported by the wonderful community of Mukim Lamunin. Set in the picturesque backdrop of the Tutong forest, our little abode caters to those looking for a reprieve from the hustle and bustle of everyday life.

We invite visitors to wake up to the sound of birds chirping early in the morning, inhale crisp fresh air and bask in the beauty of nature surrounding the area while enjoying what we have to offer at our little quaint garden.

Peace, serenity and tranquility - are just a few steps away at Eco Ponies Garden.

We look forward to seeing you here!
